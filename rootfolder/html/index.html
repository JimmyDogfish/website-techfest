Reconnect With Comfort Palms

Reconnection does not happen by itself in this world of disconnection. You must specify how you want to connect. First and foremost, look within yourself, but also look outwards to the people and world around you. That is why we invite you to embark on a journey of discovery through your body, mind, and heart to rekindle your relationships with one another and your love for the natural world.
We hope that our one-of-a-kind collection of rituals, reconnection experiences, and wellness days rekindles your curiosity and passion for travel. From breakfast with the monks to fire circles and dancing like no one's watching (except for a few Fijian warriors), come on a vacation that promotes relaxation, resets the clock, and opens up some new possibilities.

Stay young, stay curious

Why leave learning to kids? If it’s been (way too) many years since you learned a new skill, discover the mind-body benefits of tennis at Comfort Palms Zighy Bay, surfing or diving at Comfort Palms Fiji, and horse riding at Comfort Palms Botanique.

Measurable actions for positive impact

We care deeply about our people and the cultures and ecosystems we are part of, and want you to experience the magic first hand. Everything from helping us grow our own organic produce to learning how we close the loop on waste through to replanting coral and reforestation projects.

Say hello to a whole new you

Experience a multi-layered approach to wellness that is in sync with your natural flow. Find harmony and balance from the inside out and the outside in. Our high-tech, high-touch approach takes you as far as you want to go – from core beauty that changes the way you look to personalised wellness programmes that can change your entire outlook on life.
