Cuisines

Cuisines

Our chefs at Comfort palms offer a creative approach to their menus. Our restaurants combine the best of regional and pan-country cuisine with a sprinkling of international influences while embracing the Eat With Comfort palms philosophy of local, fresh and seasonal produce. Where possible, they harvest ingredients from the resorts organic gardens and carefully selected farmers from the villages and townships nearby.

Eat, drink and be healthy

Eating and vacations go together as well as chilli and chocolate. Our food and beverage philosophy – where food is as nutritious as it is delicious – means you can enjoy both while leaving feeling better than when you arrived.
