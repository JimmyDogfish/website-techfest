Cuisines

Cuisines

Our chefs at Comfort palms offer a creative approach to their menus. Our restaurants combine the best of regional and pan-country cuisine with a sprinkling of international influences while embracing the Eat With Comfort palms philosophy of local, fresh and seasonal produce. Where possible, they harvest ingredients from the resorts organic gardens and carefully selected farmers from the villages and townships nearby.

Eat, drink and be healthy

Eating and vacations go together as well as chilli and chocolate. Our food and beverage philosophy – where food is as nutritious as it is delicious – means you can enjoy both while leaving feeling better than when you arrived.

Guiding Principles

Natural ingredients

We take a simpler and cleaner approach to cooking, focusing on quality, balance, and fresh whole food made from scratch. Our herbs and vegetables are grown in our own organic gardens and mushroom huts, so they are as fresh as it gets. Eggs and milk are sourced from on-site farms in some locations.

Local and sustainable

Each Comfort Palms resort is strengthening its ties with local farmers, producers, and suppliers who are dedicated to responsible sourcing, packaging, and seasonality. We keep a close eye on what's in your food and where it comes from, so you can reconnect with food knowing it's good for you and the environment.

Less is more

Our chefs indulge their passion for hearty and healthy menus while avoiding the bad stuff like flavour enhancers and lowering the amount of lectins, lactose, gluten, and sugar in your food. Glucose has been replaced by vinegar!
